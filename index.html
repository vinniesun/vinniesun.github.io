Pao-Sheng Vincent Sun

I'm a PhD Student at City University of Hong Kong under the supervision of Professor Arindam Basu.

We are part of the BRAINSys Lab.

My research is on Neuromorphic Computing, with a focus on Spiking Neural Network and Event Vision.

Before I started working on my PhD, I've worked at Everlight Electronics and Dynasafe Technology, both based in Taiwan.
